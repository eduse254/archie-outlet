About ARCHIE'S OUTLET

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, eco-friendly cleaning solutions that promote health, sustainability, and value for our customers and communities.

Our Purpose

ARCHIE'S OUTLET exists to make everyday cleaning safer, more effective, and environmentally responsible through innovative products and dedicated service.

Our Vision

To be the leading supplier of natural soaps and detergents in Kenya and beyond, recognized for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

  • Integrity & Transparency
  • Quality & Innovation
  • Environmental Responsibility
  • Customer Focus
  • Community Engagement

Commitment to Quality

Every product is formulated by hand, tested for safety and performance, and shipped with care. We offer a range of products and customized formulations to ensure optimal results and cost-effectiveness for every customer.

Our Process

We use plant-based cleansers and sustainable practices to craft small-batch liquid soaps and detergents. Our team is dedicated to continuous improvement and innovation in all we do.
